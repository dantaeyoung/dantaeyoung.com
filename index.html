Things I am thinking about lately (as of 2019-02):

Physical space is a computational medium. Physical space computes things. The sound that a doorbell makes in the space is a social/spatial computation that balances proximity and attention.

We are our environments. Our cognition is distributed in the environment. We are the relationships and spaces that surround us.

A culture of 'professionalism' is predicated on a denial of gender, race, and the body (and power structures that makes it possible for some to deny these.).
Avoiding professionalism culture is to find healthier ways of thoughtful, insightful action by being in our bodies.
